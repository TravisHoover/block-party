# Block Party — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 8, 2026

Block Party is a simple offline puzzle game.

## What we collect

Nothing. Block Party does not collect, store, transmit, or share any
personal information or usage data of any kind.

- **No ads.** The app contains no advertising and no ad networks.
- **No tracking.** The app contains no analytics, tracking, or
  third-party SDKs.
- **No accounts.** The app has no sign-up, sign-in, or user accounts.
- **No network access.** The app never connects to the internet.

## Data stored on your device

Your high score and settings are saved only on your own device, and are
deleted if you delete the app. They never leave the device.

## Children's privacy

Block Party collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on this
project's GitHub repository.
